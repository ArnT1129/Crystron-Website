Venus3: The Next Leap in Battery Tech

Our proprietary Venus3 technology redefines Cathode Active Material (CAM) manufacturing, overcoming the limitations of traditional LFP and NMC processes while embracing sustainability and performance.

Traditional LFP Challenges

  • Complex & Inefficient: Multi-step pCAM synthesis from FeSO4 & H3PO4.
  • High Operational Costs: Requires extreme temperatures (800°C for 6-8 hours), leading to massive energy use.
  • Environmental Impact: Generates 1-2 tons of Na2SO4 waste per ton of CAM, contaminating ecosystems.
  • Supply Chain Vulnerabilities: Often reliant on imported materials and complex logistics.

The Venus3 Advantage

  • Simplified & Direct: Proprietary Li-salt reaction with an iron-based compound, eliminating pCAM steps.
  • Drastically Lower Costs: Up to 40% reduction via efficient flow, domestic raw materials, and smaller equipment footprint.
  • True Circularity: 81% lower energy & emissions, zero water consumption, and NO Na2SO4 or other harmful waste. Solvent is recycled.
  • Enhanced Performance: Potential for improved CAM characteristics leading to better battery life and efficiency.

The Venus3 process: a paradigm shift in CAM production for a sustainable future.

Key Technical Features

  • Novel Synthesis Route: Patented low-temperature reaction.
  • Material Purity: High-purity CAM output for optimal performance.
  • Scalability: Designed for rapid and cost-effective industrial scale-up.
  • Versatility: Adaptable for various iron-based CAM chemistries.
  • Reduced Carbon Footprint: Significantly lower cradle-to-gate emissions.

Potential Applications

  • Electric Vehicles (EVs): Longer-lasting, more affordable batteries.
  • Grid-Scale Storage: Reliable and sustainable energy storage solutions.
  • Consumer Electronics: Higher performance batteries for everyday devices.
  • Industrial Equipment: Powering heavy-duty machinery efficiently.
  • Renewable Energy Integration: Optimizing solar and wind power storage.
