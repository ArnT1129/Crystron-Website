A Leap in Cathode Manufacturing

Our proprietary technology redefines Cathode Active Material (CAM) manufacturing, overcoming the critical limitations of traditional processes while championing sustainability and performance.

The Problem with Conventional LFP Production

Complex Supply Chain

Reliant on many input materials, the supply of which is dominated by Chinese companies, creating vulnerabilities.

Inefficient Manufacturing

Involves multiple energy-intensive steps that result in sub-optimal throughput and higher operational complexity.

High Manufacturing Cost

Requires a large equipment footprint, high electricity consumption, and numerous input materials, driving up CAPEX and OPEX.

Significant Environmental Impact

Generates high volumes of toxic waste coupled with high water and electricity consumption, harming ecosystems.

Our Solution: A Clear Advantage

  • Simpler Supply Chain: No pCAM needed. Uses fewer input materials that are abundantly available as by-products of steel & electrolyte making.
  • Efficient Manufacturing: A streamlined process with fewer steps leads to significantly higher throughput and reliability.
  • Lower Manufacturing Cost: Reduced equipment footprint and low electricity consumption drive >40% savings in both OPEX & CAPEX.
  • Zero Environmental Impact: Our circular process features zero waste generation, zero water consumption, and full solvent recycling.
